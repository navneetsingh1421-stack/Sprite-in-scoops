Our Story

Sprit in Scoop was born from a moment of pure dessert genius. During a fancy dinner date, we wondered: what if we combined the refreshing fizz of Sprite with the creamy delight of ice cream?


The result? A dessert revolution! Perfect carbonation meets perfect creaminess in every bite. Since our first creation, we've been serving this magical combination to delighted customers at friend reunions, romantic dinners, and special celebrations.
